About

Welcome! This website contains pet customizers created by me, BrightSunshine, for use with the game Animal Jam Classic. They're completely safe and free to use, but please look over the Usage Policy below for my conditions before continuing. I'm always working on adding more so stay tuned! ^-^


Navigation & Features

Use the respective buttons to select features for the pet. Click the "Save" button to download a transparent version of your custom pet, or click the "New Tab" button towards the right to open the picture of the pet in a new tab. Remember to rotate to horizontal if you're on mobile for easier viewing!


Usage Policy

You can freely use these customizers and the pet files generated from them as long as you use them appropriately, credit me (such as leaving the watermark untouched), and don't attempt to profit off of my work in any way. Thanks for understanding!
